Frequently Asked Questions

Got any questions? Maybe they can be answered below!

NEWHow do I earn my Refer-A-Friend reward?

To earn your $50 reward, your referred friend must complete the following within 90 days of setting up their Trading Account:

1. Make a first-time deposit of at least USD $100
2. Trade a minimum of 1 standard lot

Once your friend completes both tasks, you each receive $50.

Who is eligible to participate in the Refer-A-Friend program?

1. This promotion is only open to registered users from SEA and ROW regions.
(View all eligible countries in our Terms and Conditions)

2. The referee must not have any existing account with TMGM.

3. Both Referrer & Referee are non-IB clients of TMGM.

To participate, login into your TMGM Account and head over to the Refer-A-Friend Tab and Click on "Invite Friends". Once you have gathered your referral link / QR Code, you're ready to share it to your friends or through other methods like Social Media.

Can I refer friends who are already customers? How many friends can I refer?

No, you can't refer friends who are already TMGM customers as they will be considered invalid. However, you can refer to as many friends as you want, as long as they do not have a TMGM Account and are a non-IB client of TMGM.

How do I know if my friend has signed up or completed the required actions?

Login into your account, find the Refer-A-Friend tab and click on "Invite Friend". Once inside, the details about the user who joined AND their status will be shown there!

What happens if two people refer to the same friend?

If two people refer to the same friend, that friend will have to choose which referral link / QR Code they want to use to sign-up with.

Scenario 1: Referrer A & Referrer B sends referral links to Friend C. Friend C decides they want to use Referrer A's referral link to complete their sign-up. Friend C will be under Referrer A.

Scenario 2: Referrer A & Referrer B sends referral links to Friend C. Friend C decides to click on Referrer A's link however decides to not proceed to sign up; they then click on Referrer B's link and continues sign up using their link. Friend C will then be under Referrer B instead.

Can I instantly withdraw my rewards?

Yes, you can! You can instantly withdraw your rewards as soon as you receive them!
